Prototype Write-up

So far, our team has coded initial prototypes for all five sections of the recommender-system explorable explanation, although only four of these sections are currently visible on the webpage. We already implemented interactive visuals for the Recommendation System Overview, Rating Matrix, Similarity Calculations, and Personal Recommender Demo, each using D3 and embedded within our custom Netflix-styled interface. Even though the collaborative filtering section is not yet displayed, its core logic, animations, and user-selection interactions have already been prototyped and are in the process of being integrated into the site. At this stage, everything is still an early prototype, but we plan to continuously iterate on the visuals, refine transitions, and polish the layout to make the final experience more intuitive and visually appealing.

The most challenging part of our project will be getting all five interactive sections to work seamlessly within the skeleton Netflix-style UI that we designed. Each visualization currently works in isolation, but ensuring that they interact smoothly with the overall page structure (while maintaining good performance and visual coherence) will require careful coordination and redesign. Integrating the unfinished collaborative filtering section will be especially tricky, since it involves animations, dynamic user inputs, and explanatory text that must fit naturally into the existing layout without overwhelming the viewer. Because we want the final site to feel polished, cohesive, and engaging, bringing every component together into a unified interface will be the most technically demanding and design-intensive part of the project.
